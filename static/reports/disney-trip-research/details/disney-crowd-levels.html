Disney World Crowd Levels & Best Times to Visit (2026)

Research compiled: February 26, 2026

1. Monthly Crowd Level Ratings

Composite ratings based on data from multiple crowd calendar sources (Ziggy Knows Disney, Walt Disney World Prep School, Magic Guides, Disney Tourist Blog, MickeyVisit). Scale: 1 = very low crowds, 10 = extreme crowds.

Month Crowd Level (1-10) Summary
January 3-4 Crowded Jan 1 and marathon weekend (Jan 7-11). Drops significantly after MLK weekend. Jan 20-30 is a sweet spot.
February 4-5 Generally moderate. Presidents Day weekend (Feb 13-22) spikes to high. Princess Half Marathon weekend (Feb 26-Mar 2) adds crowds.
March 7-8 Spring break season begins. Mid-March through end of month is very busy. Early March (Mar 1-8) can still be reasonable.
April 6-8 Easter and spring break continue through mid-April. Cheerleading Worlds (Apr 24-27) spike late April. Pockets of calm exist mid-month.
May 4-5 Moderate early month. Memorial Day weekend (May 22-25) is busy. May 4-21 weekdays are a top pick for low crowds.
June 6-7 Summer crowds build. Florida schools out mid-May, northern schools out early June. Hot and increasingly crowded.
July 7-8 Peak summer. 4th of July week is one of the busiest of the year. High heat compounds the experience.
August 3-5 Crowds taper dramatically mid-month. Aug 10-31 is among the lowest crowd periods all year. Extreme heat is the trade-off.
September 2-3 Lowest crowds of the year. Labor Day weekend is the exception. After Labor Day through month-end is the best time for low waits.
October 5-6 Crowds build through the month. Halloween parties draw visitors. Wine & Dine Half Marathon weekend (Oct 22-25). Columbus Day weekend busy.
November 4-7 Early Nov (1-13) is moderate. Thanksgiving week (Nov 20-29) is very crowded. Veterans Day weekend also busy.
December 5-9 Early Dec weekdays (1-11) are moderate. Dec 19-Jan 1 is the busiest period of the entire year.

Sources: - Ziggy Knows Disney — Best Time to Visit - Walt Disney World Prep School — Crowd Calendar - Magic Guides — Crowd Calendar - MickeyVisit — Crowd Calendar - Disney Tourist Blog — Best & Worst Weeks

Summary: Top Recommended Windows

Rank Window Why
1 Sep 7-30 Lowest crowds, cheapest tickets, biggest discounts, Food & Wine Festival. Trade-off: extreme heat.
2 Aug 19-31 Very low crowds, cheap tickets, free kids' dining. Trade-off: hottest month.
3 Jan 20-30 Low crowds, cool weather, Festival of the Arts. Trade-off: some rides may be in refurbishment.
4 May 4-21 (weekdays) Moderate crowds, Flower & Garden Festival ending. Trade-off: heating up, storms starting.
5 Early Nov (1-13) Low-moderate crowds, Food & Wine wrapping up, pleasant weather. Trade-off: less likely to have room discounts.
6 Early Mar (1-8) Excellent weather, Flower & Garden Festival starts, low crowds before spring break. Trade-off: brief window.
7 Early Dec (1-11 weekdays) Holiday decorations up, Festival of the Holidays, moderate crowds. Trade-off: narrow window before Christmas rush.

The "sweet spot" depends on your priorities: - Lowest crowds + cheapest: Mid-September (but very hot) - Best weather + low crowds: Late January or early March - Best balance of everything: Early November — pleasant weather, moderate crowds, great festival, reasonable prices

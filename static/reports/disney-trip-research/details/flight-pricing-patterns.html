Research date: February 26, 2026

3. School Vacation Week Impact

Massachusetts February Vacation (Presidents Day Week)

  • 2026 dates: February 16-20 (Presidents Day is Feb 16)
  • Price impact: Fares during this week typically run 30-50% higher than the surrounding weeks in February. February is normally one of the cheapest months, but this week is the exception.
  • Strategy: Book 40+ days in advance. Consider flying out Saturday Feb 14 (before the rush) and returning the following Saturday Feb 21.

Massachusetts April Vacation (Spring Break)

  • 2026 dates: April 20-24 (most MA districts)
  • Price impact: Spring break to Florida is one of the highest-demand travel periods from the Northeast. Expect fares 40-100% above off-peak April prices. Data shows NYC-to-Miami fares hit $400-500 round trip during spring break vs $250-300 off-peak -- similar premiums apply to BOS-MCO.
  • Strategy: If possible, travel the week before (Apr 13-17) or after (Apr 27-May 1) for significant savings. If locked into vacation week, book 6-8 weeks in advance.
  • 2026 positive note: Economy domestic airfare for spring break 2026 is trending 3.5% cheaper than 2025 (TravelPulse).

General Vacation Week Premium

School vacation weeks from the Northeast to Florida carry a 40-100% fare premium over adjacent off-peak weeks. The premium is highest for: - February vacation (concentrated New England demand) - April spring break (overlaps with many states' breaks) - Thanksgiving week - Christmas/New Year

Sources: Dollar Flight Club, Going.com, TravelPulse

BOS to SFB (Orlando Sanford)

  • No nonstop flights from Boston to Sanford (SFB). Allegiant Air flies to SFB from many smaller airports but does not serve BOS-SFB nonstop.
  • Allegiant does fly nonstop from Bangor (BGR) and Portsmouth (PSM) to SFB if those are options.
  • SFB is about 45 min northeast of Disney World (vs ~25 min from MCO).

  • Distance: ~1,121 miles

  • Season: Shoulder/value season
  • Expected round trip per person: $150-230 (budget carriers: $90-160)
  • Family of 4 round trip estimate: $600-920
  • Outlook: Good value period. Halloween at Disney is popular but doesn't spike airfare as much as school vacation weeks. Early November is generally affordable.
  • Strategy: Book 5-6 weeks ahead (mid-September). Saturday Oct 31 departure will cost more than a Wednesday — consider departing Fri Oct 30 evening.

7. Tips for Saving

Fare Alerts & Flexible Date Tools

  • Google Flights price tracking: Toggle "Track prices" for your route/dates. Get email alerts when prices drop. Use the calendar view to spot the cheapest departure/return dates within a range. (Google Support)
  • Going.com (formerly Scott's Cheap Flights): Premium membership sends deal alerts for routes you specify. Has caught BOS-MCO deals as low as $54 round trip.
  • Hopper app: Predicts whether to buy now or wait. Color-coded calendar shows cheapest dates.
  • KAYAK price alerts: Set up for your specific route and dates.

Additional Money-Saving Tips

  1. Fly midweek (Tue/Wed) — saves up to 52% vs peak days
  2. Choose evening departures — average $37 less than morning flights
  3. Compare budget carriers carefully — Spirit/Frontier base fares are cheap but add bags ($35-65 each way per bag), seat selection ($5-30), and no free snacks/drinks. For a family of 4 with checked bags, JetBlue or Southwest may be cheaper all-in.
  4. Book on Sunday — 6-13% cheaper than booking on Friday
  5. Use Google Flights "Explore" — enter BOS, leave destination blank, pick your dates, and see a map of cheapest destinations (useful for confirming Orlando is the best value vs other Florida airports)
  6. Consider one-way pricing — mix airlines for outbound/return (e.g., Spirit outbound + JetBlue return) to optimize total cost
  7. Southwest = free bags — Even if the fare is $20-40 more, Southwest's 2 free checked bags (up to 8 bags for a family of 4) can save $200+ compared to other carriers
  8. BOS parking tip — Off-airport parking (e.g., Park Shuttle & Fly) can save $100+ over a week vs on-airport garages

2025-2026 School Year

Break Dates Notes
February Vacation (Winter Recess) Mon Feb 16 – Fri Feb 20, 2026 Full week off. Lunar New Year (Feb 17) falls during break.
Spring Recess (April Vacation) Mon Apr 20 – Fri Apr 24, 2026 Full week off. Good Friday is Apr 3.
Thanksgiving Recess Thu Nov 27 – Fri Nov 28, 2025 2 days off (Wed Nov 26 is early dismissal).
December Recess Wed Dec 24, 2025 – Wed Dec 31, 2025 ~6 school days off (Dec 24-31).

Park Overview

Disney's Hollywood Studios is the most ride-dense park at Walt Disney World, with a heavy concentration of headline attractions. It leans toward older kids and adults more than any other Walt Disney World park, making it an outstanding fit for a family with a 10-year-old thrill-seeker. The park is organized around themed "lands" inspired by movies and TV, with Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge and Toy Story Land as the two most immersive areas.

Park size: Smallest of the four Walt Disney World parks by acreage, but packed with high-demand rides. Typical visit length: Full day (10-12 hours recommended to see everything). Early Theme Park Entry: 30 minutes before official opening for Disney resort guests.

All Attractions by Land

Hollywood Boulevard

Attraction Type Height Req. Thrill Level Avg. Wait Best For
Mickey & Minnie's Runaway Railway Dark ride / trackless None Gentle-Moderate 45-70 min Whole family -- colorful, whimsical, no scary moments. Daughter will love the cartoon world.
Hyperion Theater (coming) Future show venue -- -- -- TBD

Mickey & Minnie's Runaway Railway is located inside the replica Chinese Theater. It uses trackless ride vehicles and projection-mapped scenes to pull you into a zany Mickey Mouse cartoon. No height requirement makes it ideal for the whole family. The 2.5D effects and unexpected vehicle movements delight kids of all ages.

Echo Lake

Attraction Type Height Req. Thrill Level Avg. Wait Best For
Star Tours -- The Adventures Continue Motion simulator 40" Moderate 20-35 min Son (Star Wars + motion). Random scene combos mean every ride is different.
Indiana Jones Epic Stunt Spectacular! Live stunt show None Gentle (watching) Show schedule (30 min) Son and Dad -- explosions, fight choreography, audience volunteers.
For the First Time in Forever: A Frozen Sing-Along Celebration Stage show None Gentle Show schedule (30 min) Daughter -- Frozen songs, funny narrators, snow effects

Star Tours randomly combines scenes from across all Star Wars eras, so you can ride multiple times and get a different experience. The 40" height requirement means both kids can ride.

Indiana Jones Epic Stunt Spectacular is a 30-minute outdoor show recreating iconic scenes from Raiders of the Lost Ark with real fire, explosions, and fight choreography. Audience members can volunteer as extras. Seats ~2,000; rarely a long wait if you arrive 15-20 minutes early.

Frozen Sing-Along retells the Frozen story with two hilarious "Royal Historians of Arendelle" narrating between musical numbers. The entire audience sings along. Your daughter will be in heaven. Shows run multiple times daily; check the schedule.

Mickey Shorts Theater shows a 10-minute original Mickey Mouse cartoon with 4D effects (wind, scents, water). Low wait, air-conditioned, fun palette cleanser between bigger attractions.

Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge

Attraction Type Height Req. Thrill Level Avg. Wait Best For
Star Wars: Rise of the Resistance Dark ride / trackless + drop 40" Moderate-Thrilling 60-120 min Son (absolute must-do) -- but whole family will be amazed. Most ambitious Disney ride ever built.
Millennium Falcon: Smugglers Run Motion simulator / interactive 38" Moderate 40-70 min Son (pilot the Falcon!). New Mandalorian mission launches May 22, 2026.
Savi's Workshop -- Handbuilt Lightsabers Interactive experience None (recommended 5+) Gentle Reservation required Son -- builds his own lightsaber ($274.99 + tax). Emotional, immersive ceremony.
Droid Depot Interactive experience None Gentle Reservation required Son (and daughter may enjoy too) -- build a custom R2 or BB droid ($129.99 + tax).
Character encounters Meet-and-greet None Gentle Varies Whole family -- Chewbacca, Rey, Kylo Ren, and Stormtroopers roam the land in-character

Rise of the Resistance is widely considered the best ride at Walt Disney World, period. It combines a trackless dark ride, a drop sequence, walk-through sets, and animatronics into an 18-minute experience. Both kids exceed the 40" requirement. The ride is prone to occasional breakdowns, so ride it early.

Smugglers Run puts you in the cockpit of the Millennium Falcon in a crew of six. Pilot position is the most fun (push buttons, steer). The new Mandalorian mission (May 2026) replaces the Hondo storyline with Din Djarin and Grogu.

Savi's Workshop is a 20-minute guided ceremony where your son would build a custom lightsaber from one of four kyber crystal themes (Peace & Justice, Power & Control, Elemental Nature, Protection & Defense). Reservations are essential -- book as soon as your 60-day window opens. Price is steep at $274.99 but it is a core memory for Star Wars kids.

Droid Depot lets kids build a BB-unit or R-unit droid from parts on a conveyor belt. At $129.99 it is more accessible than lightsabers. The droids interact with elements around Galaxy's Edge.

Toy Story Land

Attraction Type Height Req. Thrill Level Avg. Wait Best For
Slinky Dog Dash Family coaster 38" Moderate 50-80 min Whole family -- smooth, fun coaster with great theming. Not too intense for daughter, still fun for son.
Toy Story Mania! Interactive 3D shooter None Gentle 30-55 min Whole family -- competitive, replayable. Dad vs. son shootout.
Alien Swirling Saucers Spinning flat ride 32" Gentle-Moderate 20-35 min Daughter -- colorful aliens, fun spinning, great music. Think "teacups in space."

Slinky Dog Dash is the land's headline. It is a family coaster (no inversions, no big drops) themed as a toy roller coaster built by Andy. Smooth enough for most 7-year-olds but still satisfying for a 10-year-old. Best ridden at rope drop or via Lightning Lane -- waits can hit 90+ minutes midday.

Toy Story Mania uses 3D glasses and spring-action shooters to play carnival games. Every family member can compete for the highest score. No height requirement and the queue is indoors (air-conditioned).

Alien Swirling Saucers is a spinner set to an electronic remix of "You've Got a Friend in Me." Short ride duration (~90 seconds) but the 32" height requirement means both kids easily qualify.

Character meet-and-greets in Toy Story Land: Woody and Bo Peep near Toy Story Mania entrance; Jessie near Alien Swirling Saucers; Buzz Lightyear near Slinky Dog Dash.

Sunset Boulevard

Attraction Type Height Req. Thrill Level Avg. Wait Best For
The Twilight Zone Tower of Terror Drop ride 40" Thrilling 30-55 min Son (and thrill-seeking parents). Randomized drop sequences. Iconic.
Rock 'n' Roller Coaster Launching indoor coaster 48" Thrilling 45-75 min Son (if tall enough -- 48" req.). 0-57 mph launch, 3 inversions, in the dark.
Fantasmic! Nighttime spectacular None Gentle (show) Amphitheater seating Whole family -- water, fire, projections, fireworks, characters
Beauty and the Beast -- Live on Stage Stage show None Gentle Show schedule (25 min) Daughter and Mom -- classic musical numbers, gorgeous costumes

Tower of Terror is the most iconic Hollywood Studios attraction. The pre-show builds incredible atmosphere (Twilight Zone theming), and the randomized drop sequences mean you never know when or how far you will fall. Both kids meet the 40" requirement. This is a must-do for the 10-year-old.

Rock 'n' Roller Coaster -- CRITICAL 2026 NOTE: The Aerosmith version closes permanently on March 2, 2026. If your trip is before March 2, ride it. If after, it will be closed until summer 2026 when it reopens as Rock 'n' Roller Coaster Starring The Muppets. The ride hardware (launch, inversions, track) stays the same -- only the theme, preshow, and soundtrack change. The 48" height requirement remains. Your son must be at least 48" tall to ride. The Muppets version will feature Dr. Teeth and the Electric Mayhem music and a chase across Hollywood.

Beauty and the Beast -- Live on Stage is a 25-minute condensed Broadway-style show at the Theater of the Stars (outdoor covered amphitheater). Classic songs, elaborate costumes. Your daughter will love it. Low-effort -- just show up 15 minutes early.

Animation Courtyard (Becoming "The Walt Disney Studios Lot" -- Summer 2026)

Attraction Type Height Req. Thrill Level Avg. Wait Best For
Walt Disney Presents Walkthrough exhibit + film None Gentle Walk-in Parents -- Disney history museum with models, art, and a short film
Disney Junior Play & Dance Interactive stage show None Gentle Show schedule CLOSED Sept 2025; replaced by Mickey Mouse Clubhouse Live! (summer 2026)

Walt Disney Presents is a hidden gem that most guests skip. It is an air-conditioned walkthrough museum of Walt Disney's life and career, ending with a short film about upcoming Disney projects. Good for a quiet midday break, especially for parents who appreciate Disney history. Previews of upcoming attractions are often shown here.

Fantasmic! Dining Package

Book a meal at one of five participating restaurants (Brown Derby, 50's Prime Time, Sci-Fi Dine-In, Hollywood & Vine, or Mama Melrose's) and receive a voucher for reserved seating at Fantasmic! This is the best way to guarantee good seats and combine dinner with the nighttime show. Reserved section is first-come, first-served within the section -- arrive 25+ minutes before showtime.

Nighttime

Fantasmic! is the park's signature nighttime event. The amphitheater seats 6,900 with room for 3,000 standing. Tips: - For the best free seats, arrive 45-60 minutes before showtime - The Fantasmic Dining Package guarantees reserved center-section seating - If two shows are offered, the second show is significantly less crowded - Sitting in the far left/right sections gives a less optimal view -- aim for center - There is no traditional fireworks show at Hollywood Studios; Fantasmic is the nighttime event

Tips & Strategy

Rope Drop Strategy

The #1 rule: Do NOT rope drop a ride you already have a Lightning Lane for. Use rope drop and Lightning Lane to cover different attractions.

Option A: Rise of the Resistance First (Recommended for this family)

  1. Arrive 45-60 minutes before official park opening
  2. If you have Early Entry (resort guests), head straight to Rise of the Resistance (Galaxy's Edge)
  3. After Rise, walk to Smugglers Run (should be 20-30 min wait at this point)
  4. Exit Galaxy's Edge and head to Slinky Dog Dash OR use your Lightning Lane Multi Pass for it
  5. Midday: Shows (Frozen Sing-Along, Indiana Jones, Beauty and the Beast)
  6. Afternoon: Tower of Terror, Toy Story Mania, Star Tours
  7. Evening: Fantasmic!

Option B: Toy Story Land First

  1. Arrive early, head straight to Slinky Dog Dash
  2. Then Toy Story Mania and Alien Swirling Saucers (knock out the whole land)
  3. Head to Galaxy's Edge for Rise of the Resistance (use Lightning Lane Single Pass if available)
  4. Continue with Smugglers Run
  5. Afternoon: Shows + Sunset Boulevard rides
  6. Evening: Fantasmic!

Lightning Lane Strategy

Pass Type Hollywood Studios Recommendations Price Range
Lightning Lane Multi Pass Tier 1: Slinky Dog Dash. Tier 2: Toy Story Mania + Tower of Terror $25-35/person
Lightning Lane Single Pass Rise of the Resistance (worth it to avoid 90+ min wait) $15-25/person
Lightning Lane Premier Pass Only if money is no object. Covers everything. $189-429/person

Key Multi Pass strategy: You choose 1 Tier 1 and 2 Tier 2 selections in advance. Best combo: - Tier 1: Slinky Dog Dash (hardest to get low wait otherwise) - Tier 2: Tower of Terror + Toy Story Mania - Rope drop: Rise of the Resistance (since it is not on Multi Pass -- it is Single Pass only)

Galaxy's Edge Tips

Tip Details
Book Oga's Cantina at 60 days Reservations open at 6:00 AM ET, 60 days before your visit. Set an alarm. They sell out fast.
Book Savi's Workshop at 60 days Same booking window. $274.99 per lightsaber. Only one builder per reservation; one guest can accompany.
Book Droid Depot at 60 days $129.99 per droid. Slightly easier to get than Savi's.
Pilot position on Smugglers Run If son wants to pilot (he will), ask to be in the front two seats when boarding. Pilots have the most interactive experience.
Interact with the land If you buy a lightsaber or droid, carry them around Galaxy's Edge -- cast members react to them in character.
Mobile order food Use the My Disney Experience app to mobile order at Docking Bay 7 and Ronto Roasters to skip the line.
Visit at night Galaxy's Edge is spectacular after dark -- the lighting transforms the land. Lower crowds in the last 2 hours.
Batuu etiquette Cast members are in character. They say "bright suns" (good morning) and "till the spire" (goodbye). Kids love this.

General Hollywood Studios Tips

Tip Details
Best day to visit Tuesdays and Wednesdays typically have the lowest crowds. Avoid weekends and any day adjacent to a holiday.
Rider Swap If daughter is too scared for Tower of Terror or Rock 'n' Roller Coaster, use Rider Swap so both parents can ride without waiting twice. Son rides with one parent; the other waits with daughter, then swaps.
Midday break Hollywood Studios gets HOT. Consider returning to your resort 12-2 PM for pool time (your wife will appreciate this), then returning for afternoon shows and Fantasmic.
Fantasmic 2nd show If two performances are offered, always pick the second. It is dramatically less crowded.
Mobile order everything Quick service lines can be 30+ minutes. Always mobile order through the app.
Charge your phone You will use the My Disney Experience app constantly. Bring a portable battery pack.
Construction awareness In early-mid 2026, portions of the park near former Muppets Courtyard will have construction walls. This does not affect any operating attractions but limits some walkways.

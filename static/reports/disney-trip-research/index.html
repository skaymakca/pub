Executive Summary

This report covers 11 research areas to help plan your family's Disney World trip. Here are the headline recommendations:

Topic Recommendation
When to go April 16-24, 2026 (extended spring break, miss 2 school days)
Flights Delta morning flights, BOS→MCO, book by early March (~$1,000-1,600 family round trip)
Why now Daughter (7) is at peak magic for princesses + arts activities; son (10) is perfect for thrill rides + sports experiences. Waiting gains nothing.
Where to stay Beach Club via Disney Vacation Club (DVC) rental (~$300-350/night vs $638+ rack rate)
How many days 5 park days: Magic Kingdom (2), Hollywood Studios (1), EPCOT (1), Animal Kingdom (half day)
Daily approach Rope drop → midday adventure (pool, Springs, BoardWalk, or water park) → evening return for fireworks
Passes Lightning Lane Multi Pass every day + Single Pass for TRON only (~$550 total)
Total cost ~$6,500-7,200 (tickets, dining, LL, photos) + hotel + flights
Planning help Free travel agent (The Vacationeer or Small World Vacations) + Touring Plans ($20/yr)

Estimated all-in trip cost: ~$9,500-11,000 including flights and hotel (Moderate resort) or ~$10,000-12,000 (Beach Club via DVC rental).

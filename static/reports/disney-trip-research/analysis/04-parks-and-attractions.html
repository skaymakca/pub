Recommended Day Allocation (5-Day Trip)

Day Park Rationale

Day 2 Hollywood Studios (full day) Rope drop Rise of the Resistance; Galaxy's Edge; shows; Fantasmic! at night
Day 3 EPCOT (full day) Rope drop rides; World Showcase opens 11 AM; festival food booths; Luminous fireworks

Tiered Recommendations

Tier 1: Non-Negotiable

  1. Allocate 2 days to Magic Kingdom — too much for one day, especially with princess meets
  2. Rope drop Rise of the Resistance at Hollywood Studios — it breaks down often; ride it first
  3. Book Cinderella's Royal Table at exactly 60 days out — hardest reservation; daughter's highlight
  4. See Happily Ever After fireworks — the single most memorable experience
  5. Eat at EPCOT festival booths — this is the foodie parents' paradise

Tier 2: Strongly Recommended

  1. Buy Individual LL for Mine Train, TRON, Rise of the Resistance — saves 3+ hours of waiting
  2. Book Oga's Cantina and Savi's Workshop at 60 days — sell out fast
  3. Use the split-and-reconvene strategy — both kids get their ideal experiences
  4. Plan Animal Kingdom as a half-day in 2026 due to closures
  5. Do Pandora at night — the bioluminescent effects are worth staying for

Tier 3: Nice to Have

  1. Book a Fantasmic Dining Package — guarantees good seats + dinner combo
  2. Do the Wilderness Explorers scavenger hunt at Animal Kingdom — keeps both kids engaged
  3. Visit Hollywood Studios Galaxy's Edge after dark — spectacular lighting
  4. Second Magic Kingdom day for what you missed — rides during fireworks have short waits

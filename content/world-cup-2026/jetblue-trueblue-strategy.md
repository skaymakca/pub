## Recommendation

**Option 4 (LA outbound + Philly AM roundtrip)** is the best overall strategy:

1. **Use 18,600 pts** for BOS->LAX Jun 4 (saves $270, 1.45 cpp) -- Marina + Maja fly to the baby shower
2. **Pay cash $219-$384** for LAX->BOS Jun 7 return (red-eye is cheaper but tough with Maja)
3. **Use 24,400 pts** for BOS->PHL + PHL->BOS AM flights (saves $376, 1.54 cpp) -- Marina + Luka for World Cup
4. **Total: 43,000 pts used, $646 saved, 24,443 pts remaining**

The key insight: the LAX *return* on Sunday Jun 7 is the worst points value in either trip (1.34 cpp daytime, 1.39 cpp red-eye). Pay cash for that leg and use points everywhere else.

**If school constraint can't be relaxed for Leg 6:** Drop Philly from points, go with Option 1 (full LA RT with red-eye, 50,200 pts, $708 saved). The after-5pm JetBlue BOS->PHL is 22,200 pts at only 1.35 cpp -- not worth the points.

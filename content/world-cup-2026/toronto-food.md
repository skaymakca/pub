All distances are measured from **The Westin Harbour Castle, 1 Harbour Square (downtown waterfront, near Union Station)**, the family's home base for the 2026 FIFA World Cup trip with kids aged 11 and 7. The scene runs from iconic cheap eats (peameal bacon sandwiches, Chinatown dumplings, market stalls) right up the ladder to Michelin-starred tasting rooms -- most of the food that matters for a family sits in the **$ to $$$** range and within a short walk or a 15-minute ride, with the splurges clustered into a separate destination tier. ⭐ marks standout picks. **Distances are MapKit-measured driving + walking from the hotel; still verify hours, open status, and reservation policy the day you go**, especially for the seasonal and low-confidence entries flagged below.

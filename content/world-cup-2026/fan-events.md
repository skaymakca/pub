A family trip built around three Croatia group-stage matches: **Dallas/Arlington** (Jun 15-18, England v Croatia, Jun 17), **Toronto** (Jun 20-24, Panama v Croatia, Jun 23), and **Philadelphia** (Jun 25-29, Croatia v Ghana, Jun 27). Kids are 11 and 7, so this leans toward free, kid-friendly, daytime options. Every host city's free **FIFA Fan Festival** runs Jun 11-Jul 19 and is the safest anchor in all three cities. The single most genuinely Croatian fan venues turned out to be **Adriatica Village / Zin Zen** (Dallas) and the **confirmed Jun 23 watch party at Father Kamber Croatian Park** (Toronto); Philadelphia's Croatian dimension is thinner (NFCACF reception Jun 26), since Croatia's actual diaspora gravity for this tournament sits in the DC/Alexandria metro (team base camp).

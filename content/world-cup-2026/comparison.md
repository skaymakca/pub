**If splitting across games:** LA family → Dallas. Zadar family → Toronto. Boston family → any (Philly is easiest).

## Decision Framework

**If the family of 4 can only attend ONE game:**
→ Philadelphia (Saturday, easiest, cheapest, great city for kids)

**If you want the most memorable match experience:**
→ Dallas (England vs. Croatia in an 80k air-conditioned stadium)

**If you want the best overall family vacation around the game:**
→ Toronto (walkable, great weather, diverse food, Croatian community, islands + aquarium + CN Tower)

**If Zadar family is joining:**
→ Toronto (Air Transat nonstop Zagreb→YYZ, Croatian community, easiest transatlantic trip by far)

**Possible multi-city itinerary (if attending all 3):**
- Jun 16–19: Dallas (4 days)
- Jun 19–21: Home (rest)
- Jun 22–25: Toronto (4 days)
- Jun 26–28: Philadelphia (3 days)

That's 11 days of travel over 13 calendar days — ambitious but doable if the whole family is up for it.

## Who is 3A/B/C/D/F? (Best 3rd-place team from those groups)

In the 48-team format, 8 of 12 third-place teams qualify for the Round of 32. Third-place teams always face group winners (never runners-up or each other). The specific opponent depends on which third-place teams qualify and how they're slotted into the bracket.

### Most Exciting Possible Opponents (for atmosphere & demand)

1. **South Korea** — large Korean-American community in Boston metro, passionate fans who travel well, historic World Cup pedigree (2002 semifinalists)
2. **Scotland** — the Tartan Army is legendary for traveling in huge numbers, would create an incredible atmosphere at Gillette

4. **Australia** — moderate interest, Socceroos have a following

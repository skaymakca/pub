### 2022 Historical Context — England Tickets

In 2022 Qatar, England group stage Cat 3 tickets ($69 face) were listed at **$10,217 on StubHub (~140x face value)**. England was among the most in-demand teams. However, 2022 resale was mostly on unauthorized platforms with a 5% FIFA fee (vs 15% in 2026).

### What This Means for Our Listing Price

VividSeats Cat 2 at $1,352 **includes buyer fees** (~15-20%). The seller likely receives $1,100–$1,200. On FIFA's platform, a $1,400 list price means:
- Buyer pays: $1,400 + 15% = **$1,610** (competitive with VividSeats' $1,352 all-in)
- We receive: $1,400 - 15% = **$1,190 per ticket**

**Note:** VividSeats/StubHub/SeatGeek listings are speculative — FIFA tickets are digitally locked and **cannot actually be sold on third-party platforms**. These prices are useful as demand signals but the only real transactions happen on FIFA's Resale Marketplace.

## Pricing Recommendation

**Start at $1,400/ticket.** List in the last week of April (not at marketplace reopening on Apr 2) to first observe pricing trends and movement across the platform.

| Factor | Reasoning |
|--------|-----------|
| **Why wait until late April?** | Marketplace reopens Apr 2. Waiting ~3.5 weeks lets us observe: how many listings appear, what prices move, whether Cat 2 demand matches VividSeats signals. Better to price with real data than guess. |
| **Why not higher ($1,800+)** | Buyer would pay $2,070 — 53% above VividSeats Cat 2. Too aggressive when we'll have 7 weeks of runway before the match. |
| **Why not lower ($1,100)** | Buyer pays $1,265 — slightly below VividSeats. Leaves $500+ on the table vs our recommended price. England demand supports a premium. |
| **Why $1,400** | Buyer pays $1,610 — 19% above VividSeats Cat 2 ($1,352), which is reasonable for the **only legitimate platform** where tickets actually transfer. FIFA is a monopoly — buyers have no alternative. |
| **Adjust if needed** | If no bites in 3 weeks (by mid-May), drop to $1,200 (buyer pays $1,380 — right at VividSeats parity). If sold within days, we priced too low — but the money is secured. |

## How to List (Step by Step)

1. Go to [fifa.com/tickets](https://www.fifa.com/tickets) and log into your FIFA ticketing account
2. Navigate to "My Tickets" → find England vs Croatia (Jun 17)
3. Select "RESELL/EXCHANGE"
4. Choose the 2 tickets you want to sell (keep Marina + Luka's)
5. Set price: **$1,400 per ticket**
6. Confirm listing
7. Tickets are removed from your account and held by FIFA while listed
8. When sold: FIFA transfers tickets to buyer, credits your payment minus 15% fee

**To change price:** Withdraw the listing → relist at new price. Cannot edit in-place.

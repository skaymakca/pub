## Dallas -- England vs. Croatia (Wed Jun 17, 3 PM CT)

**Who's going:** Marina + Luka
**Venue:** AT&T Stadium (80k, air-conditioned)

### Why This Game Is Special

England vs. Croatia is THE marquee match -- 2018 World Cup semifinal rematch, two huge fanbases, worldwide spotlight, Group L opener. 80,000 in an air-conditioned stadium. Luka will remember this forever.

### Highlights for Luka (11)

- **Perot Museum "Soccer: More Than a Game"** -- 10k sq ft interactive soccer science. Kick balls at trajectory stations, design jerseys, build Lego stadiums. Made for him.
- **Six Flags Over Texas + Hurricane Harbor** -- theme park + water park, both near the stadium
- **Fair Park Fan Festival** -- 35k capacity, 1M sq ft, free entry
- **The match itself** -- England is the opponent every kid wants to brag about

### Logistics Reminders

- Rental car essential (no transit to Arlington)
- 93-100°F -- plan indoor/water activities during peak heat
- Stadium is air-conditioned (relief from the heat)
- Fan Festival at Fair Park is ~25 min drive from stadium

## Toronto -- Panama vs. Croatia (Tue Jun 23, 7 PM ET)

**Who's going:** Sukru + Marina + Luka + Maja (all 4)
**Venue:** BMO Field (~30k)

### Why This City Shines

Best all-around family trip: walkable downtown, pleasant weather (75°F), diverse food scene, and a large Croatian community that will make the atmosphere feel like home. Fan Festival at Fort York is walking distance to the stadium.

### Highlights for Everyone

| Person | Don't Miss |
|--------|-----------|
| **Sukru** | St. Lawrence Market (world top 10), Distillery District, diverse restaurant scene |
| **Marina** | Walkable everything, no car stress, Toronto Islands for a peaceful day |
| **Luka** | CN Tower EdgeWalk, ROM dinosaurs, Canada's Wonderland, pickup soccer at Trinity Bellwoods |
| **Maja** | Ripley's Aquarium, Toronto Islands / Centreville amusement park, Snakes & Lattes (board game cafe, kids under 12 free) |

### Top Experiences

- **Niagara Falls day trip** -- full day, Niagara City Cruises boat tour is the #1 must-do ($43/$28 CAD)
- **CN Tower** -- observation deck + EdgeWalk for the brave
- **St. Lawrence Market** -- Saturday morning is the experience
- **Fan Festival at Fort York** -- walking distance to BMO Field, free, big screens
- **Germany vs. Côte d'Ivoire (Sat Jun 20, 4 PM)** at Toronto Stadium -- watchable at Fan Festival

### Logistics Reminders

- Porter flights land at Billy Bishop (YTZ) -- downtown, 10 min to hotel
- Best weather of the three cities (75°F, similar to a nice Boston day)
- Canadian Dollar ~$0.72 USD -- everything is ~28% cheaper than it looks
- Avis Brookfield Place (161 Bay St) best for Niagara day trip rental
- CityPASS available (~$363 for family, saves ~$190)

## Philadelphia -- Croatia vs. Ghana (Sat Jun 27, 5 PM ET)

**Who's going:** Marina + Luka + FIL (+ Sukru TBD)
**Venue:** Lincoln Financial Field (69k)

### Why This City Works

Easiest logistics of the three: Saturday game, short flight or drivable from Boston, no car needed, compact walkable city. SEPTA subway direct to the stadium. Plus it's America's 250th anniversary -- special exhibitions and events everywhere.

### Highlights for the Group

| Person | Don't Miss |
|--------|-----------|
| **Marina** | Reading Terminal Market (80+ vendors), Rittenhouse Square neighborhood walks, compact walkable city |
| **Luka** | Franklin Institute (Giant Heart walk-through, earthquake simulator), Independence Hall, Eastern State Penitentiary (Al Capone's cell) |
| **FIL** | Independence Hall + Liberty Bell (free), Reading Terminal Market, Rittenhouse Square park |
| **Sukru** (if going) | Zahav (modern Israeli, one of America's best), Barnes Foundation (181 Renoirs), Philadelphia Museum of Art, East Passyunk dining |

### Food Scene

- **Reading Terminal Market** -- 80+ vendors, everyone picks their own. Soul food to Pennsylvania Dutch to Asian.
- **Cheesesteaks** -- Pat's, Geno's, or Jim's (the eternal debate)
- **East Passyunk Ave** -- Le Virtu (Abruzzese BYOB), The Victor Cafe (servers sing opera between courses)
- **Zahav** -- Michael Solomonov's modern Israeli. Michelin Recommended.
- **3 Michelin-starred restaurants** (awarded Nov 2025): Her Place Supper Club, Friday Saturday Sunday, Provenance

### America's 250th Anniversary (2026)

Philadelphia is ground zero for the celebrations:
- **"A Nation of Artists"** at PMA -- 1,000+ American works, opens April 2026
- **PAFA** (Frank Furness building) freshly reopened April 2026
- **ArtPhilly festival** -- 30+ exhibitions citywide
- Independence Hall and Liberty Bell will have special programming

### Logistics Reminders

- SEPTA Broad Street Line from Center City to NRG Station -- 20 min, $2.50/pp
- Game is Saturday -- no weekday schedule stress
- Element Downtown hotel includes free breakfast + full kitchen
- FIL arrives 49 min after M+L -- meet at baggage claim, share ride to hotel

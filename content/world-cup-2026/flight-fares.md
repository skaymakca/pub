## World Cup Pricing Context

- Domestic US flights between WC host cities are seeing **25-100% surcharges** over normal summer fares
- A typical NY-DFW fare of $180-250 is running $400-600 on World Cup dates
- Canada routes (e.g. Calgary-Toronto) are roughly **double** normal summer pricing
- American Airlines is the **Official North American Airline Supplier** of FIFA World Cup 26 and has added ~27,000 seats across 12 routes (June 18 - July 11)
- AA is specifically adding capacity on BOS-DFW and upgauging aircraft on PHL-YYZ during the tournament
- **Bottom line:** Book ASAP -- every week of delay shrinks inventory and raises prices

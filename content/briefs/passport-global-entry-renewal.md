## 1. Passport Renewal for Minors (Luka & Maja)

### Requirements

Children under 16 **cannot renew by mail**. Must apply in person using **Form DS-11** (Application for a U.S. Passport).

**Documents needed per child:**
- Completed Form DS-11 (use the online form filler at travel.state.gov to avoid errors)
- Current/expired passport (will be returned by mail after processing)
- One passport photo (2"×2", strict 2026 compliance — see Tips section)
- Proof of U.S. citizenship (birth certificate — original or certified copy)
- Proof of parental relationship (birth certificate listing both parents)

**Parental presence:**
- **Both parents must appear in person** with the child at the acceptance facility
- Each parent must bring a valid photo ID (driver's license or passport)
- If one parent cannot attend: the absent parent must provide a notarized **Form DS-3053** (Statement of Consent) plus a photocopy of their ID

## 2. Global Entry Reapplication

### Why New Applications (Not Renewals)

All four memberships expired 1-2 years ago, which means the **24-month grace period has passed**. You must file entirely new applications — not simple renewals. The process and fees are the same as a first-time application.

### Fees

| Family Member | Fee |
|---------------|-----|
| Sukru (adult) | $100 |
| Marina (adult) | $100 |
| Luka (minor under 18, parent is member) | **$0** |
| Maja (minor under 18, parent is member) | **$0** |
| **Total** | **$200** |

Minors under 18 are **fee-exempt** when a parent/guardian is an active member or is applying concurrently.

### Application Process

1. Go to **https://ttp.dhs.gov** and create/log into accounts for all four family members
2. Complete a new application for each person (not renewal)
3. Pay fees by credit/debit card ($100 each for adults, $0 for minors)
4. Wait for **conditional approval** (timeline varies — can be days to weeks)
5. Schedule in-person interview at an enrollment center
6. Attend interview with required documents
7. Final approval arrives **2-3 weeks after interview**

**Parental consent** is required for minors under 18.

**Known Traveler Number (KTN):** Your existing KTN stays the same. Update it in airline frequent flyer profiles once reapproved. TSA PreCheck still works with existing KTN even while GE is expired.

**CBP assistance:** 1-877-227-5511

### Boston Logan Enrollment Center

- **Address:** 1 Harborside Drive, Terminal E, Boston, MA 02128
- **Location:** Arrivals area, first floor, right of Dunkin' Donuts
- **Phone:** (617) 568-1810
- **Hours:** 7:00 AM - 3:00 PM, Mon-Fri
- **Appointments only** — no walk-ins
- **Current wait for appointments:** ~27 days (as of March 2026), but often books out 3+ months

**Documents needed for interview:**
- Valid passport (or permanent resident card)
- One additional ID (e.g., driver's license)
- TTP conditional approval letter

### Enrollment on Arrival

Boston Logan participates in **Enrollment on Arrival**. If the family takes any international flight into Logan after receiving conditional approval:
- Tell the passport control officer you're conditionally approved and want "Enrollment on Arrival"
- Complete the interview right there — no separate appointment needed
- This is the fastest path if you have any international travel coming up

A DHS funding lapse beginning February 14, 2026 has **suspended Global Entry arrival processing** at all airports. However:
- TSA PreCheck lanes remain operational
- Your KTN still works on boarding passes
- Monitor news for when GE processing resumes

## 3. Canada Travel Requirements

### Passport Rules for US Citizens

- Valid US passport **required** for air travel to Canada — no exceptions
- **Canada does NOT enforce the 6-month validity rule** for US citizens — your passport is valid up to its printed expiration date
- No Canadian visa or eTA needed for US citizens
- Stays under 180 days do not require a tourist visa

### Requirements for Minors

- All children traveling by air must have their own valid US passport
- **If traveling without both parents present:** Canadian Border Services Agency (CBSA) requires a **signed letter of authorization** from both parents/guardians
  - Letter should include: parents' names, address, phone numbers; child's name and date of birth; name, address, and phone of the adult(s) accompanying the child in Canada
  - **Recommendation:** Have the consent letter **notarized** to avoid delays at the border
- If both Sukru and Marina are traveling with the kids, no consent letter is needed

## 4. Tips & Tricks

### Passport Photo Compliance (Critical in 2026)

New strict enforcement since January 2026 — non-compliant photos are the #1 cause of processing delays:
- White or off-white background only
- No glasses (even clear ones)
- Neutral facial expression, both eyes open
- Face centered, head fills 50-69% of frame height
- No shadows on face or background
- Taken within the last 6 months
- **Tip:** Many acceptance facilities (including Newton Center PO) take photos on-site — this guarantees compliance

### Appointment Hacks

- **USPS scheduler** (usps.com/scheduler) opens appointments on a rolling basis — check early morning for new slots
- **Newton Center PO** is the closest facility — call 617-244-2351 to confirm availability
- **Watch for passport fairs** — the State Department holds special passport acceptance events with new locations added weekly (check travel.state.gov)

### Private Expediting Services (Backup Plan)

Legitimate companies registered with the State Department that hand-carry applications:
- **RushMyPassport** (rushmypassport.com) — starts at $119 + govt fees, can process in days
- **Urgent Passport Services** (urgentpassport.com) — 3 business days + FedEx overnight
- **CIBTvisas** — as fast as 1-3 days
- **Passport Visas Express** — a week or less
- Cost range: **$119-$500+** on top of government fees

**Warning:** Do NOT pay any company claiming to book passport agency appointments — these are likely fraudulent.

### Global Entry Interview Hacks

- **Cancellation trackers:** Use services like globalentryalerts.com, ttpappointments.com, or appointmentscanner.com to get notified when earlier interview slots open at Boston Logan
- **Enrollment on Arrival** is the best option if you have any international trip before June 23
